Sustainability

Join us in our mission to protect the planet one cup at a time. Pearly Cups are designed not just with style in mind, but sustainability. By choosing our reusable cups equipped with metal straws, you're actively reducing the waste produced by disposable cups and single-use plastics that often end up in our oceans, harming marine life and polluting the earth. The environmental impact of these plastics is devastating, affecting water quality and wildlife across the globe. Every year, over 8 million tons of plastic waste escape into the oceans. This devastating amount is equivalent to dumping a garbage truck full of plastic into the waters every minute, leading to the deaths of 1 million marine animals annually. It's not just plastic bottles and straws, disposable cups and containers add significantly to this toll. Choose a Pearly Cup, reduce your impact, and help keep our oceans alive!
